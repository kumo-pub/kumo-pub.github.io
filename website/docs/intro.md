# What is kumo

Let's discover **Kumo in less than 5 minutes**.

## overview
These days, AI large models are everywhere, yet their core C++ infrastructure underneath is outdated and messy.
Kumo is built for the AI era — a unified, simple, and ultra‑fast industrial‑grade foundation.
It empowers small teams to deploy enterprise‑class search, recommendation, and AI capabilities in just two weeks, boosting industry efficiency tenfold.
